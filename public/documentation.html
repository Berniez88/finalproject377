Introduction Filler Content

The purpose of our project is to aid students on deciding on what courses to take, and with who. Often not a lot of students have a difficult time deciding on what course to take. Factors such as average grades received in the class, amount of homework given level of difficulty, along with comments for each professor is beneficial when selecting what courses to take. Our goal is to combine all these factors into one platform, making it easier for students to decide on how they want to be taught. We want to have these statistics (grade average, rating of the courses, level of difficulty, etc.) all posted next to the specified course and professor. This way students know what they’re getting themselves into, without needing to worry about the professor over assigning work or explaining work poorly.
